EASY SCHOOL

Step 1

To set up a system for your institute, create an account by filling the essential details like institute name, address, administrator name etc.

Step 2

Select a template from the available solid colored templates which best suits your institute name and logo. Check out some of the features below.

Step 3

Select the appropriate package according to your needs. A trial version of 6 months is available for testing, along with 1 year and 5 years packages.

Virtual Library

The system comes with an integrated library management system. This is open for students and the staff as well. With this, they have online access to know which books are currently available in the library. Users can choose to be informed if a book becomes available. Check out the demo on the left in full screen mode.

Online Lecture

The system provides an easy way to set up online-lectures. One-to-many video, which shares the Professor's video to all the students who have joined in.

Attendance Status

Students can access their detailed attendance status online. Staff can can take attendance of a particular class with their smartphones or laptops with ease due to the responsive design.
